
## Ingredients
- [Pretzels](https://www.amazon.com/Snyders-Hanover-Pretzel-Snaps-46/dp/B0858J4BQF?th=1&linkCode=ll1&tag=mommakmon-20&linkId=a1517522b6701b53a17c7ca6aad48edd&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)
- Butter
- [Everything But the Bagel Seasoning](https://www.amazon.com/Everything-Seasoning-Unpretentious-Texture-Convenient/dp/B07Q2CPMLX?th=1&linkCode=ll1&tag=mommakmon-20&linkId=a3a8cab2063d79be90cf530f85469986&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)
## Directions
1. Preheat the oven to 325F to begin.
2. Place the pretzels in a large bowl with enough room to mix them around in without making a huge mess.

![here we see the pretzels with seasoning almost ready to be enjoyed.](https://moneywisemoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/everything-bagel-pretzels-1-of-1-12-683x1024.jpg.webp)

3. Melt the butter, and pour it over top of the pretzels. Stir to coat, it’s important that they’re all covered so that you can get the seasoning to stick to the pretzels.
4. Sprinkle the everything bagel seasoning over the pretzels, stirring to coat everything evenly.
5. Pour the pretzels out onto a baking sheet and distribute in an even layer.
6. Bake for 10 minutes until the butter has been absorbed.
7. Allow to cool completely before storing or eating…they’ll be very toasty!
