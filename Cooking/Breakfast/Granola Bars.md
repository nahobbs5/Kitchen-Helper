### Ingredients

- 1 cup [very smooth creamy natural peanut butter](https://www.amazon.com/365-Everyday-Value-Organic-Unsweeteend/dp/B074H5ZHVZ?crid=189QZ0PH74G44&keywords=creamy%2Bnatural%2Bpeanut%2Bbutter&qid=1691094972&s=grocery&sprefix=creamy%2Bnatural%2Bpeanut%2Bbutter%2Cgrocery%2C95&sr=1-8&th=1&linkCode=ll1&tag=loveandlemobl-at-rc-ingli-20&linkId=b381e635a2c199ab2a2bb94e48376a25&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl) , or [cashew butter](https://www.amazon.com/365-Everyday-Value-Cashew-Butter/dp/B074H6R4YF?&linkCode=ll1&tag=loveandlemobl-at-rc-ingli-20&linkId=90fafd4c045a2451f0fe1456ea770d0e&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)
- ⅔ cup [honey](https://goto.target.com/c/2773249/81938/2092?subid1=5c62da580a04d93936608c49&subid2=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.loveandlemons.com%2Fgranola-bars-recipe%2F&sharedid=Love%20and%20Lemons&subid3=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.target.com%2Fp%2Fnature-nate-39-s-100-pure-raw-unfiltered-organic-honey-8211-16oz%2F-%2FA-53499918%3Faflt%3Dcse&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.target.com%2Fp%2Fnature-nate-39-s-100-pure-raw-unfiltered-organic-honey-8211-16oz%2F-%2FA-53499918%3Faflt%3Dcse#donotlink)
- 1 teaspoon [vanilla extract](https://www.amazon.com/Nielsen-Massey-Madagascar-Bourbon-Vanilla-Extract/dp/B0000VLU0I?th=1&linkCode=sl1&tag=loveandlemobl-at-rc-ingli-20&linkId=9271f43080204693719b28a432b5fdb2&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)
- Heaping ½ teaspoon [sea salt](https://www.amazon.com/365-Everyday-Value-Salt-Fine/dp/B074J7X1DW?&linkCode=sl1&tag=loveandlemobl-at-rc-ingli-20&linkId=8f6a13f00ff77c4b50139a7ddcd95527&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)
- 2½ cups [whole rolled oats](https://goto.target.com/c/2773249/81938/2092?subid1=5c62da580a04d93936608c49&subid2=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.loveandlemons.com%2Fgranola-bars-recipe%2F&sharedid=Love%20and%20Lemons&subid3=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.target.com%2Fp%2Fbob-39-s-red-mill-gluten-free-organic-old-fashioned-rolled-oats-32oz%2F-%2FA-85793926%3Faflt%3Dcse&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.target.com%2Fp%2Fbob-39-s-red-mill-gluten-free-organic-old-fashioned-rolled-oats-32oz%2F-%2FA-85793926%3Faflt%3Dcse#donotlink)
- ¼ cup [mini chocolate chips*](https://goto.target.com/c/2773249/81938/2092?subid1=5c62da580a04d93936608c49&subid2=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.loveandlemons.com%2Fgranola-bars-recipe%2F&sharedid=Love%20and%20Lemons&subid3=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.target.com%2Fp%2Fenjoy-life-semi-sweet-gluten-free-dairy-free-vegan-mini-chocolate-chips-10oz%2F-%2FA-14924784%3Faflt%3Dcse&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.target.com%2Fp%2Fenjoy-life-semi-sweet-gluten-free-dairy-free-vegan-mini-chocolate-chips-10oz%2F-%2FA-14924784%3Faflt%3Dcse#donotlink)
- 3 tablespoons [pepitas](https://www.amazon.com/365-Everyday-Value-Organic-Pumpkin/dp/B07GL6JLZY?&linkCode=sl1&tag=loveandlemobl-at-rc-ingli-20&linkId=2c09d3c3681345d6caefa52b1159d982&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl), or crushed peanuts or cashews

### Instructions

1. Line an [8x8 baking pan](https://www.amazon.com/Ateco-3-Inch-Professional-Square-Baking/dp/B004VMGCXM?th=1&linkCode=sl1&tag=loveandlemobl-at-rc-instr-20&linkId=8aeff6791aa78270f5da615fb9abfee3&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl) with [parchment paper](https://www.amazon.com/If-You-Care-Unbleached-Greaseproof/dp/B001T6JTMY?th=1&linkCode=sl1&tag=loveandlemobl-at-rc-instr-20&linkId=61b6af334f41d8802e1127740b6ffba8&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl).
    
2. In a [large bowl](https://www.amazon.com/Pyrex-Tempered-1-5-Quart-Microwave-Dishwasher/dp/B00LGLHUA0?&linkCode=ll1&tag=loveandlemobl-at-rc-instr-20&linkId=303e3693e9b02405e3ade345db84222e&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl), stir together the peanut butter, honey, vanilla, and salt, until smooth.
    
3. Add the oats, chocolate chips and the pepitas (or nuts). The mixture might seem dry at first, but keep stirring and it'll come together. Stir to combine and press firmly into the pan. Use a second piece of parchment paper and the back of a measuring cup to help flatten the mixture. Chill for at least 1 hour, then slice into bars.
