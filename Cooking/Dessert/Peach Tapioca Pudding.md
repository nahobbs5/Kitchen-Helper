## Ingredients (serves ~12 small cups)
- 6 cups chopped peaches
- ½ cup sugar
- 2 cans coconut milk
- ½ cup fine cornmeal
- Pinch salt
- Optional: ¼ cup small tapioca pearls
- Optional: mint garnish
## Steps
1. Simmer peaches + sugar until soft.
2. Add coconut milk + salt.
3. Slowly whisk in cornmeal.
4. Simmer 8–10 min until thickened.
5. If using tapioca, add pre-soaked pearls and cook until translucent.
6. Pour into cups and chill.
7. Next day or after a few hours: add small mint leaves
