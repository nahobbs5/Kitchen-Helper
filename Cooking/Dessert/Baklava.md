(Greek)
## Ingredients
### Pastry
- 1 package phyllo dough (thawed)
- ¾–1 cup melted butter
- 3 cups finely chopped walnuts
- Optional flavor (instead of cinnamon):
- 1 tsp lemon zest or orange zest
- or 1 tsp vanilla
### Honey Syrup
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup honey
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- strip of lemon peel (optional)
## Instructions
1. Make syrup first
In a saucepan combine:
- water
- sugar
- honey
Bring to a boil, then simmer 10 minutes.
Add lemon juice and peel at the end.
Let cool completely.
2. Mix filling
Combine:
- chopped walnuts
- lemon/orange zest or vanilla (optional)
3. Assemble (easy layering)
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
Butter a 9×13 pan.
Layer:
8 sheets phyllo, buttering each
All the walnut filling
8 more sheets phyllo, buttering each
That’s it — much faster than traditional layering.
4. Cut
Before baking, cut into diamonds or squares.
5. Bake
Bake 40–45 minutes until deep golden.
6. Add syrup
Pour the cool syrup over the hot baklava immediately after baking.
7. Rest
Let sit 4–6 hours before serving.
## 💡 Flavor ideas instead of cinnamon
Lemon zest (very Greek)
Orange zest
Vanilla
A tiny splash of rosewater or orange blossom water
