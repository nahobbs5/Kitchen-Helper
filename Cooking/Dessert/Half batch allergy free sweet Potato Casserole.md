
**nut-free, oat-free, dairy free, gluten free)**

### Base

- **1 ½ cups** cooked mashed sweet potatoes
- **¼ cup** sugar
- **¼ cup** unsweetened applesauce** (replaces flax egg)
- **2 tbsp** dairy-free butter substitute _(or vegetable oil)_
- **¾ tsp** vanilla extract
- **¼ tsp** salt (to taste)
- **¼ cup** unsweetened coconut milk
### 🧁 Streusel-Style Crumb Topping

- **¼ cup** gluten-free flour (Bob’s Red Mill 1-to-1 or King Arthur Measure for Measure)
- **2 ½ tbsp** brown sugar
- **⅛ tsp** cinnamon
- **2 tbsp** melted dairy-free butter or coconut/vegetable oil (if vegetable oil, **1 ½ tbsp**)

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **350°F (175°C)**.
2. In a large bowl, mix sweet potatoes, sugar, applesauce, butter or oil, vanilla, salt, and non-dairy milk until smooth.
3. Spread evenly into a **greased 8×8-inch baking dish**.
4. In a small bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, and cinnamon. Stir in melted butter or oil until crumbly.
5. Sprinkle topping evenly over the sweet potato mixture.
6. Bake uncovered for **25–30 minutes** until the topping is golden brown and lightly crisp.
