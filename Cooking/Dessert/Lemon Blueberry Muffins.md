
Here’s a **simple, allergy-friendly lemon blueberry muffin recipe** that is:

- **Egg-free**
- **Dairy-free**
- **Nut-free**
- **Soy-free**
- Easily made **gluten-free** if needed

---

## 🌼 **Allergy-Free Lemon Blueberry Muffins**

### **Ingredients (makes ~12 muffins)**

**Dry ingredients**

- 2 cups all-purpose flour  
    _(or a 1:1 gluten-free baking blend if needed)_
- ¾ cup sugar
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- Zest of 1 large lemon

**Wet ingredients**

- 1 cup non-dairy milk (oat, almond, soy, etc.; oat works best for texture)
- ⅓ cup neutral oil (**canola, vegetable, or melted coconut**)
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice (several Lemons)
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

**Add-ins**

- 1–1 ½ cups fresh or frozen blueberries  
    _(if frozen, do **not** thaw—toss with 1 Tbsp flour to prevent color bleeding)_

---

## 🍋 **Instructions**

1. **Preheat** oven to 400°F (205°C). Line a muffin tin.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together:
    - flour
    - sugar
    - baking powder
    - salt
    - lemon zest
3. In a separate bowl, whisk:
    - non-dairy milk
    - oil
    - lemon juice
    - vanilla
4. Pour wet ingredients into dry and stir **just until combined**.  
    (Batter should be thick — don’t overmix.)
5. Fold in blueberries gently.
6. Fill muffin cups almost to the top.
7. Bake **18–22 minutes**, until lightly golden and a toothpick comes out clean.
8. Cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a rack.

---

## 🌟 Tips for Best Results

- **Extra lemony?** Add another teaspoon of zest or a bit of lemon extract.
- **Sweeter?** Increase sugar to 1 cup.
- **Gluten-free version:** Use King Arthur or Bob’s Red Mill GF 1:1 and add 2 extra tablespoons of milk if the batter seems too thick.
- **Moister muffins:** Add 2 Tbsp applesauce (still egg-free).
